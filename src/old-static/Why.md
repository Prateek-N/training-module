# Onboarding Compliance: Why It Matters 🛡️

Welcome to the team! Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and Compliance Guidelines are not just rules—they are the **shield** that protects our candidates, our experts, and the reputation of our company. 

This guide explains the **"Why"** behind our policies so you can understand their importance and champion them daily. 🤝

---

## 1. Why Compliance is Our Shield 🛡️
Compliance ensures consistency and excellence. When every expert follows the exact same playbook, we eliminate human error and deliver a seamless experience.
* **Client Trust**: Clients depend on us for error-free support. A single slip-up can ruin months of built-up trust.
* **Expert Safety**: Following the SOP ensures you are fully protected in case a candidate makes a false complaint.
* **Unified Success**: Our operations run smoothly when every wheel turns in sync.

> Deviation from compliance—especially missing mandatory items—makes the expert fully responsible if a complaint arises. Stay compliant, stay safe! ⚠️

---

## 2. The 30-20-10 Rule: Why Time is of the Essence ⏰
We require check-in calls at **30, 20, and 10 minutes** before every session. Here is why:
* **The 30-Minute Check**: Confirms the candidate is awake, active, and sitting at their desk. If they don't answer, it gives us an early warning to escalate.
* **The 20-Minute Check**: Verifies that their internet connection is stable and the Google Doc workspace is ready.
* **The 10-Minute Check**: The final check. We verify that their camera, microphone, and screen share are fully functional.

> Waiting until the last minute is a recipe for disaster. Technical issues take time to resolve. The 30-20-10 rule gives us a critical buffer! 🕰️

---

## 3. Tech Preparation: Why Audio & Screen Checks are Mandatory 🎧
Testing screen and audio sharing before the session starts is non-negotiable.
* **Avoid Disqualification**: Many assessment platforms immediately flag candidates if they configure screen sharing during the test. Pre-testing prevents this.
* **Ensure Smooth Support**: Real-time support relies on hearing the interviewer clearly. We play a test audio sample to ensure the routing works perfectly.
* **Eliminate Pauses**: Testing beforehand ensures there are no long, awkward delays during the live session.

---

## 4. Session Recording: Why Visual Proof is Non-Negotiable 📹
We require a full video recording of every assessment support session.
* **Evidence of Support**: In case of a dispute, the recording is our only concrete evidence that we provided the support as promised.
* **Quality Assurance**: Recordings help managers review sessions, offer feedback, and help you improve your support technique.
* **Default Accountability**: If a session has no recording, the expert is held responsible by default if any issues arise. Always double-check that your recording is active!

---

## 5. Remote Support: Why Candidate Experience Comes First 💻
When assisting candidates, we must match their tone and provide help naturally.
* **Layman-Style Explanations**: Candidates must be able to explain the logic naturally to the interviewer. We avoid text-book jargon and use example-based answers.
* **Coding Protocol**: We use clear, clean code with single-character variables (unless it is SQL) to keep the screen uncluttered and easy for the candidate to explain.
* **Active Monitoring**: If remote access is denied, the expert must still monitor and point out syntax errors in real-time on the shared document.

---

## 6. Honest Documentation: Why Live Updates & Anti-AI Policies Exist 📝
Writing detailed feedback and updating documents in real-time is crucial for team alignment.
* **No Vague/One-Liner Feedback**: Vague notes like "it went well" are useless for tracking a candidate's progress. We need specific details on what was asked and what was answered.
* **Strict Anti-AI Policy**: Using AI to generate feedback is strictly forbidden. AI feedback lacks human insight, misrepresents actual session metrics, and ignores candidate behavior.
* **Live Doc Updates**: Updating the Google Doc during the interview ensures that if another expert needs to step in or review, they have the exact current state immediately.

---

## 7. Zero Tolerance: Why We Stand for Absolute Accountability 🚨
Our Zero-Tolerance Policies exist because compliance lapses have severe consequences.
* **Client Cancellations**: A single missed support session can lead to a client cancelling their contract, affecting the livelihood of the entire team.
* **Consequences of Lapses**:
  * **1st Infraction**: Verbal Warning
  * **2nd Infraction**: Written Warning & Task Restrictions
  * **3rd Infraction**: Management Escalation & Pool Removal
  * **3 Warnings in a Month**: Half-day salary deduction.

> Remember, compliance isn't about restriction—it's about protecting our candidates, our teammates, and our collective success. Thank you for your commitment to excellence! 🌟
